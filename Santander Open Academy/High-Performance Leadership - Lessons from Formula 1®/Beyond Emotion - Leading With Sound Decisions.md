## Decision Making under pressure

1. Remove emotion from the process.
	- What does the logic tell me?
	- What are the facts?

2. Get the Right Perspectives.
	- Seek data driven insights, not just gut feelings.

3. Commit to the decision fully.
	- Leadership is knowing when to **fight, adapt or let go**.

## From Doubt to Dominance

People who have spent every moment of their lives working for a job still suffer from self-doubt. Self-doubt can lead to many unintended consequences - like eschewing new challenges, procrastinating on decisions, second guessing yourself, losing your motivation and anxiety.

The Cambridge Dictionary defines impostor syndrome as "the feeling that your achievements are not real or that you do not deserve praise or success".

### Managing Doubt

- Prepare.
	- Revision.
	- Relearning.
	- Refinement.
	- Reinforcement.

- Don't wait.
- Stay calm.
