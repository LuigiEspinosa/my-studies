### Veeva CLM

Veeva CLM enables closed-loop marketing to allow a flow of content and data through interactions with customers.

### Veeva CRM Approved Email

Approved Emails are HTML emails created to allow the Rep to send emails within Veeva CRM in approved, compliant and trackable manner.

Approved Emails are highly customizable and can enable Reps to share content, follow up on consent, send invitations, and much more, with all email interactions captured within Veeva CRM.

### Veeva CRM Engage Meeting

Engage Meeting enables Reps to hold remote meetings sharing compliant CLM presentations to HCPs in real time with video/webcam and audio.

HCPs can join an Engage Meeting from any device, with all interactions captured back to Veeva CRM for a seamless remote detailing experience.

### Veeva CRM Engage for Portals

Engage for Portals enables pharma to re-use CLM content within their websites for HCPs to review in their own time, while capturing data from each interaction back into Veeva CRM.

With Engage for Portals, CLM content can be embedded into any website including brand portals and company websites.

### Veeva CRM MyInsights

MyInsights enables pharma to have HTML data visualizations within any Veeva CRM device to assist field teams with access to easy, undestandable insights.

MyInsights uses HTML and JavaScript to gather real-time information from Veeva CRM into a visual report. Reps can the use the most current data for better field execution.

## Veeva Vault PromoMats

### Commercial Content

Veeva Vault PromoMats provides a single solution for compliant content, content creation and digital asset management (DAM).

It supports medical, legal, regulatory, brand and marketing teams to speed up the creation, review and distribution of content, whilst ensuring that content is compliant.

Companies can share and re-use content more easily across global entites and channels.

### Supporting the End-to-End Conetnt Journey

Vault PromoMats is the single source of truth for commercial content, from creation to distribution.

It handles content review, approval, multichannel distribution, digital asset management and performance analytics.

> [ Create -> Review -> Approval -> Share -> Publish ] <- Analyze Content Performance.

### Review and Approval

Vault PromoMats is a could-based solution built specifically for the life sciences industry to speed up compliant content review.

Content review and approval can be completed in unison with real time collaboration acorss medical, legal and regulatory teams.

Vault PromoMats supports the substantiations of promotional claims with the correct evidence, which can be automated using AI to scan a library of claims and create the reference annotation.

### Digital Asset Management (DAM)

Its capabilities, including Brand Portal and Digital Publishing, provide a repository for content that promotes content reuse across teams, local and global.

- Presentations
- Web Assets
- Artwork
- References
- Components
- Photos

### Brand Portal

Provides an e-commerce like user experience that allows brand materials to be highlighted and organized in a way that other markerts and agencies can easily find content and component assets and use them accordingly.

Marketing teams can improve global brand alignment and increase content reuse.

### Digital Publishing

Content can be published to digital channels through an integration with a customer-owned Amazon Web Services (AWS) environment that provides one-click web publishing of assets in a compliant manner.

This ensures changes to content are automatically reflexted in the correct digital channel, including the withdrawal of content when it expires. This integration also allows for metrics on content usage.

### Analytics

Identifies and reports on where claims and content are in use and their status in the review and approval prcess. This helps to minimize bottlenecks and allows information to be easily displayed in a dashboard for analysis within the solution.

Customers can benchmark their content performance based on actual data.

### Vault PromoMats as a Multichannel Repository

In addition to supporting the full end-to-end journey for commercial content for all channels, Vault PromoMats is the repository for all Multichannel Content. CLM, Approved Email and Engage content are loaded into Vault to allow Content Creators to test in Veeva CRM in the same manner as Customers manage their content.

> Content -> Execution -> Insights
