# Veeva CRM Engage

Veeva Engage is beyond an Engage Meeting and is about Meet, stay Connected and Share with HCPs, and is now about supporting hybrid engagements for the field. It is truly a platform that will support the business needs for all types of Engagement.

It comprises 5 main business capabilities that support HCPs and Reps in and between meetigns. It supports Field Agility to:

- Allow field teams to adapt to local, fuild access conditions (in person or remote).
- Share content or capture signatures either remotely, in person or being socially distanced.
- Provide a compliant way for field teams to chat with HCPs.

## Trace and Measure

1. Schedule: Appointment booking with HCPs, i.e., Integrated Scheduling.
2. Meet: Integrated video calls and events, i.e., Engage Meeting.
3. Connenct: Compliant chat with HCPs anytime, anywhere, i.e., Engage Connect.
4. Share: Present and send content through any channel, i.e., Send Content and Remote CLM.
5. Sign: Contactless signature capture through any channel, i.e., Engage Sign.

## Veeva CRM Engage - Capability Examples

All the interactions are traced in Veeva CRM and contribute to a better 360 view of the customer.

1. As part of Engage we have the ability to agree with the HCP how and when he or she likes to meet. Also called Integrated scheduling.

2. This is Engage Meeting capability to meet remotely 1:1, in groups or hosting an online or hybrid event.

3. Connect...
    - Is about staying connected with HCPs between meetings.
    - Through Digital Business Cards, HCPs can initiate a converstaion between themselves and Reps. It can be used to request a Sample, Content or just to raise a question.
    - Connect is also about your Company Brand Profile, allowing companies to provide easy access to Brand, patient or Reimbursement resources.

4. Allows Users to share any compliant content stored in Veeva CRM, through any channel. Its sharing a unique content link through any channel e.g. WhatsApp, iMessage or Chat.

5. Enables the user to capture a Sample or Medical Inquiry Signature. This can be done through Engage Meeting but also QR code or links.
