
## Introduction to Veeva Commercial Cloud

The Veeva Commercial Cloud provides the foundation for digital excellence, powered by Data, Multichannel CRM, and Content Management.

Veeva's products are built specifically for life sciences companies as cloud-based online applications.

The Commercial Cloud is made of multiple products which work together but can also be used individually.

It provides life sciences with the backbone to drive an omnichannel experience. In addition to the Engagement and Content products, the Commercial Cloud includes Data and Analytics products.

## What is CRM?

CRM stands for "**customer relationship management,** a process in which a business or other organization administers its interactions with customers, typically using data analysis to study large amounts of information." [^1]

[^1]: Bardicchia, Marco (2020). *Digital CRM: Strategies and Emerging Trends: Building Customer Relationship in the Digital Era. p. 12*

## What is Veeva CRM?

Veeva CRM is a CRM solution optimized for life sciences. It enables personalized customer interactions, for compliant tailored engagement across digital and personal channels.

Veeva CRM is built on top of [Salesforce.com](https://www.salesfoce.com) so customers benefit from both Veeva and Salesforce innovation.

## Veeva CRM

Veeva CRM Suite **aligns te right resources** to customers and **delivers a multichannel customer experience** for personalized interactions and better engagement.

Veeva CRM is the core product of the CRM Suite providing field teams with **full visibility of customer interactions** to drive intelligent engagement and give Rep's access to real-time data to help plan and execute calls.

Within the CRM Suite, there are **additional content-based products** that make up Veeva's Multichannel CRM.

## Veeva CLM

Veeva CLM enables closed-loop marketing to allow a flow of content and data through interactions with customers.

These are typically presentations used in a face-to-face meeting.

## Veeva CRM Approved Email

Approved Emails are **HTML emails** created to allow the Rep to send emails **within Veeva CRM** in an **approved, compliant** and **trackable** manner.

Approved Emails are **highly customizable** and can enable Reps to share content, follow up on consent, send invitations, and much more, with all email interactions captured within Veeva CRM.

## Veeva CRM Engage Meeting

Engage Meeting enables Reps to hold **remote meetings sharing compliant CLM presentations** to HCPs in **real time** with video/webcam and audio.

HCPs can **join** an Engage Meeting from **any device,** with all interactions captured back to Veeva CRM for a seamless remote detailing experience.

## Veeva CRM Engage for Portals

Engage for Portals enables pharma to **re-use CLM content** within their websites for HCPs to review in their own time, while capturing data from each interaction back into Veeva CRM.

With Engage for Portals, **CLM content can be embedded** into any website including brand portals and company websites.

## Veeva CRM MyInsights

MyInsights enables pharma to have **HTML data visualizations** within any Veeva CRM device to assist field teams with access to easy, understandable insights.

MyInsights uses **HTML and JavaScript** to gather real-time information from Veeva CRM into a visual report. Reps can then use the most current data for better field execution.

## Commercial Content

Veeva Vault PromoMats provides a **single solution for compliant content, content creation and digital asset management** (DAM).

Vault PromoMats supports medical, legal, regulatory, brand and marketing teams to **speed up the creation, review and distribution of content,** whilst ensuring that content is **compliant**.

With all content assets managed within Vault PromoMats, companies can **share and re-use content** more easily across **global entities and channels**.

## Support the End-to-End Content Journey

Vault PromoMats is the **single source of truth** for commercial content, **from creation to distribution.**

It handles content review, approval, multichannel distribution, digital asset management and performance analytics. [^4]

[^4]: Customers with PromoMats licenses may not have turned on all features, but they are supported in their license.

## Review and Approval

Vault PromoMats is a **could-based solution** built specifically for the life sciences industry **to speed up compliant content review**.

Content review and approval can be completed in unison with real time collaboration across medical, legal and regulatory teams.

Vault PromoMats supports the substantiations of promotional claims with the correct evidence, which can be automated using AI to scan a library of claims and create the reference annotation.

## Digital Asset Management

Vault PromoMats **Digital Asset Management (DAM)** capabilities, including Brand Portal and Digital Publishing, provide a repository for content that promotes **content reuse** across teams, local and global.

This is done by leveraging **already approved component assets** and **making copies** of final tactics to be localized for markets.

## Brand Portal

Brand Portal provides an e-commerce like users experience that allows brand materials to be highlighted and organized in a way that other markets and agencies can easily **find content and component assets**and use them accordingly.

With Brand Portal, marketing teams can improve global brand alignment and increase content reuse.

## Digital Publishing

Content can be published to digital channels through an integration with a customer-owned Amazon Web Services (AWS) environment that provides **one-click web publishing** of assets in a compliant manner.

This ensures changes to content in Vault PromoMats are **automatically reflected in the correct digital channel,** including the withdrawal of content when it expires.

This integration also allows for metrics on content usage.

## Analytics

Vault PromoMats **identifies** and **reports** on where claims and content are in use and **their status in the review and approval process.** This helps to minimize bottlenecks and allows information to be easily displayed in a dashboard for analysis within the solution.

Through the collection of Standard Metrics, Vault PromoMats customers can benchmark their **content performance** based on actual data.

## Vault PromoMats as a Multichannel Repository

In addition to [[#Support the End-to-End Content Journey]] for commercial content for all channels, Vault PromoMats is the repository for all **Multichannel Content**. CLM, Approved Email and Engage content are loaded into Vault to allow Content Creators to test in Veeva CRM in the same manner as Customers manage their content.

## Veeva Ecosystem for Content Partners

The Veeva content ecosystem consists of the following:

- Veeva CRM Online
- Veeva Vault PromoMats
- Veeva CRM Mobile Application (iPad or Window Device).
- Content Distribution Networks (CDN).

## Veeva Ecosystem Actions

1. **Upload** Document into Vault PromoMats.
2. **Sync** Meta Data to Veeva CRM Online.
3. **Content automatically sent** to the CDN.
4. **Download HCP and Content** data to Veeva CRM Mobile Application.
5. **Content automatically downloaded** from the **CDN**.
6. CRM Activities **synced** to Veeva CRM Online.
