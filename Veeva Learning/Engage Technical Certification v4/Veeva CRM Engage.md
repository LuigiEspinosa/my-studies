## Veeva CRM Engage Platform Overview

Veeva Engage is beyond an Engage Meeting and is about **Meet**, stay **Connected** and **Share** with HCPs, and is now about supporting hybrid engagements for the field.

It is truly a platform that will support the business needs for all types of Engagement.

It comprises 5 main business capabilities that support HCPs and Reps in and between meetings. It supports Field Agility to:

- Allow field teams to adapt to local, fluid access conditions (in person or remote).
- Share content or capture signatures either remotely, in person or being socially distanced.
- Provide a compliant way for field teams to chat with HCPs.

## Veeva CRM Engage - Capability Examples

All the interactions in Veeva CRM and contribute to a better 360 view of the customer.

### Schedule

- As part of Engage we have the ability to agree with the HCP how and when he or she likes to meet. Also called integrated scheduling.

### Meet

- This is the Engage Meeting capability to meet remotely 1:1, in groups or hosting an online or hybrid event.

### Connect

- Is about staying connected with HCPs between meetings.
- Through Digital Business Cards, HCPs can initiate a conversation between themselves and Reps. It can be used to request a Sample, Content or just to raise a question.
- Connect is also about your Company Brand Profile, allowing companies to provide easy access to Brand, patient or Reimbursement resources.

### Share

- Allows Users to share any compliant content stored in Veeva CRM, through any channel. Its sharing a unique content link through any channel e.g. WhatsApp, iMessage or Chat.

### Sign

- Enables the user to capture a Sample or Medical Inquiry Signature. This can be done through Engage Meeting but also QR codes or links.
