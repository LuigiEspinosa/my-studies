## Hosting an Engage Meeting via an iPad
### Scheduling an Engage Meeting

To schedule an Engage Meeting, the Rep needs to:

1. Select an Account and 'Record a Call' to open the Call Report page.
2. Change the record type to 'Remote Meeting'.
3. Select 'Schedule Remote Meeting' from the 'More Actions' menu.

### Copy Button for Meeting Invite Information

A Copy button has been added to both the Remote Meeting Information and Send invitations modals to simplify the process of providing meeting connection details to attendees.

### Sending Invitations

Once the Engage Meeting is scheduled, the Rep can send an invitation directly from the Call Report to the HCP using Approved Email. Approved Email Templates that are created for Engage Meeting are available via the 'Send Invitation' button.

### Approved Email Invitation

Approved Email invitations are a compliant and secure way to invite HCPs to an Engage Meeting.

Approved Email Configurations Tokens allow Content Creators to dynamically insert key information that is unique to each email invitation:

- Meeting Date and Time.
- Participant URL Link.
- Calendar Reminder (.ICS file).
- Meeting ID.
- Meeting Password.

### Sharing Engage Meeting Details Manually

While Approved Email is recommended, the meeting details can be accessed manually and copied vie the 'information' icon.

The Meeting Name can be changed manually, and a Meeting Description can be added. These will pull though to the Calendar Reminder (.ICS file) if the `{{addToCalendar}}` Token is added to the Email Template.

Additionally, the Meeting ID, Password and Participant URL can be copied to share via another channel.

### Start Engage Meetings from the Call Report - iPad

Reps have access to meeting controls, including attendee video tiles, notifications, and control over the sharing of content. Audio and video are automatically started.

### Start Engage Meetings from the Call Report - Lightning

The new 'Meet Now' button provides the ability to quickly start an Engage Meeting on the fly. This button will display on unsubmitted calls where a remote meeting has not been scheduled.

### Sharing CLM Content

Reps have access to their CLM Presentation library via the 'Media icon' and can select any presentation to share.

The yellow border on the iPad shows the Rep that the HCP does not see any content.

Once the Rep selects a CLM Presentation, the content is shared with the HCP, this is indicated by the green border.

The video ribbon is automatically displayed but can be hidden by the Rep if preferred.

The Rep will be able to see all meeting controls to Mute, Strop Video and Chat.

These controls are not visible to the HCP.

### Ending the Meeting and Submitting the Call Report

When the Rep clicks 'End Meeting' this ends the meeting for all participants and automatically displays the Call Report page for the Rep to complete and submit.

All Products and Key Messages shown during the Engage Meeting are captured in the Call Report.

## Hosting an Engage Meeting via CRM Desktop (Windows)

### Scheduling an Engage Meeting

From CRM Online on a Windows PC, the Rep can schedule an Engage Meeting by selecting an Account and then clicking 'Record a Call' to open the Call Report page.

The Rep changes the record type to 'Remote Meeting' and selects 'Schedule' to create the Engage Meeting.

### Sending Invitations

Once the Engage Meeting is scheduled, the Rep can send an invitation.

Approved Email Templates that are created for Engage Meeting are available via the 'Send Invitation' button.

### Sharing Engage Meeting Details Manually

While Approved Email is recommended, the meeting details can be accessed manually and copied via the 'information' icon.

The Meeting Name can be changed manually, and Meeting Description can be added. These will pull through to the Calendar Reminder (.ICS file) if the `{{addToCalendar}}` Token is added to the Email Template.

Additionally, the Meeting ID, Password and Participant URL can be copied to share via another channel.

### Starting an Engage Meeting

The Rep starts the meeting directly from the Call Report. The CRM Desktop application is launched, if installed. If the Rep has not installed the application previously this will prompt them to download.

Audio and video are started automatically for the Rep. Reps have access to meeting controls, including attendee video tiles, notifications, and control over the sharing of content.

The new Present in Engage button can be used to quickly share CLM content, bypassing the standard navigation where content had to opened first and then selected from the Share window.

- Content shared with the HCP is indicated with a green border.
- The Rep will be able to see all meeting controls is including muting audio, stopping video and chat. This isn't shared with the HCP.
- This feature is available in the Engage Sandbox for testing.

This feature reduces the number of clicks it takes to share content during an EM which allows the user to move faster and have less disruption in the conversation. The highlighter pen and laser pointer are not available when sharing CLM content in the CRM desktop application.

### Sharing Desktop Content

When the Rep selects 'Desktop', a window opens displaying all active applications.

The Rep can select one application at a time to share with the HCP.

Reps can switch between Desktop and Media throughout the Engage Meeting call.

Sharing from Desktop must be enable for this option to work. Sharing from Desktop is not supported when using a laptop in tablet mode.

### Ending the Meeting and Submitting the Call Report

When the Rep clicks 'End Meeting', this ends the meeting for all participants, and automatically launches the Call Report page for the Rep to complete and submit.

All Products and Key Messages shown during the Engage Meeting are captured in the Call Report.

## Sharing Content with Veeva CRM Desktop App (Windows)

There is a simplified process to share content during an Engage Meeting or Virtual Event and, in addition, CLM Content will now be available to be shown during a Virtual Event.

Once Share Screen is selected from an Engage Meeting/Virtual Event, all available CLM Content and Desktop windows will appear in one place.

- For CLM content in Normal view, the entire window is shared. Click 'Present' to move to Presentation mode and start tracking activity.
- For Presentations already in Presentation view, the app immediately starts tracking the activity.
- The existing Engage Group Admin permission for 'Disable Desktop Sharing' will continue to be respected.

## How to Share Content with Veeva CRM Desktop App

1. The Host clicks on 'Share Screen'.
2. Then selects the appropriate window, and clicks Share.
3. The content is shared.

The 'Call Mode' banner will not be shared with attendees.

## HCP Joining an Engage Meeting

- HCPs can join an Engage Meeting using the Participant URL sent to them by the Rep.
- HCPs can also join using the Meeting ID and Password if needed. When the HCP joins the Engage Meeting, an onboarding page displays, instructing the HCP how to join their meeting based on their device and browser type. Supported Devices and Browsers are:
	- Engage Web Client.
	- Veeva Engage Android app in Google Play.
	- Veeva Engage iOS app in Apple App Store.
	- Windows 7+ HCP app for desktop/laptop.
- Via iOS or Android Mobile Device.
- Via Web Client.
	- Engage Web Client is supported on Internet Explorer 11+, Edge Chromium, Chrome, Firefox and Safari.
- HCPs Joining an Engage Meeting on Windows 8+ PC, but without access to a supported browser, can join the meeting by downloading the Windows HCP Application.
	- If the Engage Application has already been installed on the HCPs desktop, then the onboarding page will show a link to open the application.

## Engage for Zoom

HCPs can join an Engage Meeting using their native Zoom application.

When enabled, Reps still schedule and host the Engage Meeting as normal, but HCPs have the choice of joining the meeting via Zoom or Engage Meeting. This makes it easier for HCPs and Reps to connect virtually, while maintaining the compliant hosting capabilities of an Engage Meeting.

Customers no longer require separate Zoom licenses if they have Engage Meeting licenses.

## Zoom Attendees in Engage Meetings

HCPs joining an Engage Meeting via Zoom have access to the same in-meeting functionality as HCPs joining via Engage Meeting Web Client or Engage HCP Application, except for 'Remote Signature Capture' and the 'Rating an Engage Meeting Experience' features.

## Sharing the Zoom URL

Like the Engage Meeting Participant URL, the Zoom URL is shared with HCPs by copying from the Meeting Details modal, or sending an Approved Email Invitation with the appropriate tokens.

## Scheduling Engage Meeting for Group Calls

Group Calls can be scheduled from both a Person Account and a Business Account in Veeva CRM.

This enables the Rep to send out the unique Participant URLs to each invited HCP, without needing to invite a Business Account.

This allows the attendance of each HCP to the meeting to be uniquely tracked.

To enable this process, the Call Report must be configured to include the '`--paa` section signal'.
