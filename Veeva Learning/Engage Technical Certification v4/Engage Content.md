Engage Content is a licensed mobile application used by end users to enable virtual connections and communication with HCPs, including the following functionality: [^1]

- Connect end users with HCPs.
- One- or two-way messaging via email or chat.
- Share approved content.
- Request Samples and materials.
- View upcoming Engage Meetings.
- Join Engage Meetings.

[^1]: This functionality can be controlled at an org-wide level, or by grouping users together. Functionality between the end user and the HCP is driven by the end user's Engage Connect Group.

## Veeva Apps Overview

### For the Field

#### Engage Connect

- Connect, chat and share content with HCPs.
- Mobile and iPad

#### Veeva CRM

- Schedule, run and record interactions (e.g., using Engage Meeting, Approved Email, etc.).
- iPhone, iPad and browser.

### For HCPs

#### Veeva Engage

- Join virtual meetings.
- View upcoming meetings.
- Connect and message with pharma.
- Obtain brand and support information.
- Mobile, iPad and browser.

## Veeva Engage Feature Overview

- **Brand Profile:** Pharma informing HCPs, e.g. patient and reimbursement resources.
- **Connections:** A 'digital business card'. Connects the HCP and Rep. Enables the HCP to reach-out to the Rep.
- **Engage Meeting:** Invite, Execute, and record.
- **Integrated Scheduling:** HCP-centric scheduling.
- **Meeting Overview:** View upcoming and historical meetings.
- **Messaging:** One, or two-way (Ping a Rep and Chat). Captured in Veeva CRM (Call Report).
- **Remote CLM:** Instant CLM Sharing.
- **Request Samples/Materials:** Requested by the HCP.
- **Share Content via Web Links:** Compliant CLM Presentations. Compliant Websites.

## Benefits

- **Increase Access and Engagement:** Create connections with HCPs automatically, and increase access in between meetings.
- **Let HCPs Easily Contact You:** Make it easy for your customers to request information and samples, view patient resources, and meet with the right people on demand.
- **Stay Compliant:** Unlick a new channel and respond to customer requests and questions in real-time with compliant and secure SMS texting.
- **Activate Field Teams:** Field representatives use the Engage Connect app to respond to all HCP inquiries and texts with compliance and ease.

## Compliant, Two-way Messaging

Pharma reps and MSLs can securely chat with HCP customers anytime, anywhere to deliver timely information on potential treatments. HCPs are also able to chat with each other.

- Deepen existing HCP relationships with customer-centric engagement and chat-like messaging.
- Respond more quickly to HCP requests for patient resources, content, or samples.
- Provide customers with the convenience and ease of a single, compliant, industry-wide pull channel.

## On-demand Content and Patient Resources

Life sciences brands can more quickly respond to HCP requests for materials, samples, patient resources, or services.

- Safely share content and resources in person, while maintaining social distance.
- Respond to requests for samples and materials quickly and collect signatures remotely.
- Enable the HCP to self serve with easy, on-demand access to brand content and resources.

## Account Deep Linking form Chat in Engage Connect

When there is a corresponding account in Veeva CRM, a button displays beside the HCP's name within the Chat tab, allowing quick navigation back to the account within the Veeva CRM application.

## HCP Centric Scheduling

Field reps and MSLs can check HCP office hour preferences and request meetings.

- Ensure more effective interactions, by selecting from HCP preferred days, times, and channels.
- HCPs can personalize their schedule to show office hours and office policies.
- Field reps can easily select from the most convenient options for their customer.

## HCP and Industry Connections

Reps and participating HCPs can instantly search and connect with each other, bringing multiple resources into one place.

- Stay connected with customers in between in-person or remote meetings.
- Consistent and easy follow up after meetings.
- Create new connections with hard-to-reach HCPs that prefer digital engagement.

## Engage Connect Groups

A new object has been added to allow the rep to create or edit settings for Engage Connect Groups via data loading Engage Connect Group Requests.

- When Engage Connect Group Request records are data-loaded, the Engage Connect server creates or updates the appropriate groups.
- The data-loaded records are automatically updated to inform users of the server request status.
