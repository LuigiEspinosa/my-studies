## Remote Engagement and Multichannel Strategy

Remote engagement is essential to a multichannel strategy. With face-to-face interactions declining, it is more important than ever for Customers to stay connected with HCPs and be able to present relevant and current information quickly.

Veeva CRM provides multiple, different channels that Customers can leverage to help their Reps engage remotely with HCPs.

- 85% of HCPs are interested in meeting online. [^1]

[^1]: Veeva Early Adopter Program

## Overview

Engage Meeting enables Reps and HCPs to connect virtually. 

Reps can schedule and host video meetings with HCPs online, and share reusable, compliant content directly from Veeva CRM.

Up to 50 Attendees (HCPs) + 1 Host (Rep) can join an Engage Meeting. Reps can host meetings and share content from an iPad or Windows PC.

HCPs can join from a dedicated application on iOS / Android / Windows, or from the Web Client via their preferred web browser, in which case no installation is required.

At a technical level, Engage Meeting securely leverages some components of Zoom's Software Development Kits (SDKs) to deliver a familiar experience for both Reps and HCPs.

## Use Cases

Engage Meeting enables Reps to:

- Engage more effectively with HCPs that restrict access or have limited availability during regular business hours.
- Expand into white space and reach HCPs across a large geographic area without the burden of travel.
- Ensure business continuity in the event of bad weather, natural disasters, and epidemics.
- Host smaller-scale group meetings.

## Benefits

- **Improve Customer Engagement** - Allows Customers to deliver the information HCPs need through a channel where they want to connect with Reps.
- **Increase Productivity and Reach** - Ensures greater coverage, frequency, and reach with the same resources.
- **Enables Content Reuse and Compliance** - Field tams stay on message, with compliance, using only approved and existing CLM content.

## Adoption

Since January 2020, there has been a huge increase in global uptake for Engage Meeting due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

The number of Engage Meetings started increased by **447%** [^2] globally.

[^2]: Engage Meetings Jan 2020 - October 2021 comparison.

## Engage Meetings with HCPs Last Longer

Engage Meeting is show to extend the average duration of a meeting, especially when engaging with HCPs regarding oncology and specialty medicine.

When the science gets more complex, the conversations go longer.

For Content Creators, this means being thoughtful in supporting Customers to adjust content accordingly.

- Face-to-Face Meeting - 2 minutes.
- Engage Meeting - 17 minutes.
- Engage Meeting in Oncology - 10 minutes

## Hosting Experience

Reps can host an Engage Meeting and share content from an iPad or Windows PC.

- **Using iPad** - Reps using iPad can host Engage Meetings directly in Veeva CRM and share CLM content with HCPs.
- **Using Windows PCs** - Reps using Veeva CRM Browser (Windows PCs) can host Engage Meetings using the CRM Desktop Application and can additionally share their desktop with HCPs (if enabled).

## HCP Experience

HCPs can join in Engage Meeting using the Participant URL sent to them by the Rep.

When an invited HCP joins an Engage Meeting using their Participant URL, an onboarding page displays instructing the HCP how to join the meeting based on their device and browser.

The appropriate Engage HCP Application download link is automatically displayed on the onboarding page when an HCP joins via an iOS or Android device.

HCPs who already have the appropriate application can select the 'Join Meting' button to join the meeting.

HCPs automatically join the meeting using the Engage Web Client unless the Windows Engage Attendee Application is installed.

HCPs joining from the Web Client must use a supported browser to join the meeting.

The Web Client is supported on the following desktop browsers:

- Internet Explorer 11+
- Edge Chromium
- Chrome
- Firefox
- Safari

## CLM Presentation

Existing Veeva CLM content can be reused in Engage Meeting, and CLM content is presented in over 80% of Engage Meeting calls.

While content can be repurposed, best practice would be to build content specifically for the channel.

Content Creators should also consider how content might need to be adapted for the longer call times seen in Engage Meeting.

## Engage Meeting invitation

Engage Meeting can be used with Veeva CRM Approved Email to send meeting invitations to up to 50 HCPs at once.

Email Templates are classified as invitations to an Engage Meeting, enabling hosts to send approved content to HCPs in advance.

Engage Meeting invitations contain meeting information, an attendee-specific URL that the HCP can use to join the Engage Meeting and calendar reminders.

## Splash Screen

A Splash Screen displays to HCPs when a Rep is not sharing content during an Engage Meeting.

These can be useful to provide company or Rep content information during the meeting.

Admins can Content Creators can customize the Splash Screen to enhance the meeting experience for HCP. 

And if a custom Splash Screen is not used, a Veeva Standard Splash Screen will be displayed.

## License and Meeting Group Permissions

User must be granted licenses [^3] in order to use Engage Meeting and Meeting Groups.

The Engage Meeting license management process enables Admins to control user assignment to Engage Groups via the User object, enabling assignment to be controlled via data-loading and user management integrations.

Admins can create new Engage Groups via the existing process on the Engage Meeting Administration tab. However, Admins are not able to rename created Engage Groups.

[^3]: Veeva Content partners can test Engage Meeting in their sandbox. Limit of 2 licenses per partner sandbox.

## Engage Meeting User Permissions

Many of the features in Engage Meeting are controlled by the group permissions in the Engage Meeting Administration Tab in Veeva CRM Online.
