## The basics of Writing using AI

The key is to make sure you're not letting it compromise the quality of your content! that means you're being purposeful and careful with how you se AI.

- It provides content ideas instantly, so you can **save a lot of time** that you'd usually spend brainstorming.
- You can easily **overcome writer's block** in seconds.
- With its speediness, you can **improve your productivity levels.**
- It can give you a **fresh perspective** that you may not have thought of before.

## Things to Consider

AI it's a robot. which means that it won't write with your unique tone of voice that matches your brand. That's why it's best to use AI as a *tool* to generate content *ideas*. rather than whole paragraphs of content.

### AI can't:

- Verify content - it may not be accurate and it cannot tell you if its accurate or not.
- Add backlinks for blogs.
- Reference any websites.
- Write accurate news articles.
- Provide accurate revisions.
- Do anything involved with a web look-up.
- Always provide unbiased content, as its underlying training data may be biased due to its restrictions.
- Always provide the most specific tone of voice you want - it can generate "funny" content, but it's just not very good at humorous or witty content.
- Avoid plagiarism.

## What it means to use AI in your copywriting

Using AI to write makes the process easier for someone who isn't a professional as is trying to start up their business.

- **Overcome Writer's block**: Ask it to generate ideas for you based on certain topics.
- **Generate Ideas**: Get it to help you come up with blog topics, social media caption ideas, landing page structures, and so on.
- **Convert a Paragraph**: When you're stuck on trying to summarize a large block of content, ask it to convert a large paragraph into a dot points for better readability.
- **Create Catchy Content**: Ask it to create catchy content and witty one-liners that don't always come to use easily, like for subject lines for emails, dynamic headlines for a landing page or blog, or slogans for your brand.

## Chat GPT vs Open AI

- Chat GPT is meant to be used as if you're "chatting" with a human on the other side.
- Chat GPT was developed by the company Open AI.
- It is easier and less complicated to use.
- With Open AI, you're able to customize the experience by tuning into a set of attributes, which makes it more complex.
- Open AI developed Chat GPT.
- It is its own entity and AI platform.

## How to use AI to Write Copy

- **Blogs**: Always do your research to make sure you're still making your blog content unique and interesting. Even though you need to stick to certain trending topics that everyone does, add your own flair to Chat GPT's content ideas with standout phrases and words in your headlines.
- **Brand Name**: Get unique and niche when talking to Chat GPT. Really focus on what type of vibe you want to come across in your name. Don't be afraid to get weird and wacky with it.
- **Social Content**: It's so easy to get stuck when trying to come up with ideas for social posts. Chat GPT can help spark your motivation with unique trends and ideas that others may not have considered.
