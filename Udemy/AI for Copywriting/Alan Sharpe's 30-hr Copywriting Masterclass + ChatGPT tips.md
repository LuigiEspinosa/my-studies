The key thing to understand about Chat GPT is that you shouldn't use it to replace you as a writer. Instead, you should learn how to use Chat GPT as an innovative tool in your writing toolbox.

You have to be aware of what it can and cannot do and where you need to step in and apply your judgment.

1. Chat GPT doesn't cite or link to sources.
2. Chat GPT doesn't provide the latest information.
3. Chat GPT makes stuff and makes errors.
